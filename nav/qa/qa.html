Placing an Order
Orders can be submitted through PayPal or CashApp. This document explains the process in more detail.
How the music in your order is selected

Our team will curate each order on an individual basis. The primary goal is to ensure that the albums blend together nicely to make a coherent whole. Each order will contain works from a variety of genres, so you won't be gridlocked into any one flavor of music.

In essence, this product is a pseudo mystery-box. "Pseudo" in that we've deliberately chosen the works that go into an order, but still making it random enough to contain a significant degree of mystery.

New or Used?

Completely assembled through second-hand sources. Used discs and used record. Everything that leaves the facility is guaranteed to meet this standard of quality.

What Choke looks for when adding to its catalog

If the listening team feels an item is compelling enough, both sonically and artistically, then it will be included. Every item that has made it onto the catalog has done so only after passing multiple listening sessions meant to gauge its "effectiveness" as best as possible.

Of course, what people find compelling varies greatly. This is where Choke entrusts its team with giving every item they analyze a fair, focused, unbiased, and appreciative listen. The hope is that this translates to music you find enjoyable and ripping.
