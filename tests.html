Genres

Because music will be exchanged between Choke and the public at a consistent rate, the contents of its catalogue is likely to change significantly in relatively short time spans. Thus, an active record of what's available can be found here. Use it for reference when placing an order.

Placing an Order
Money transfers can be made out to chokefoundation on Venmo, PayPal, or CashApp.

Indicate the following within the Note section of your money transfer:
  • Five Genre List
  • Digital / Analog / Chimera
  • Mailing Adress
  • Phone Number / Email.
    Used for communication purposes if issues arise in an order.

Once Choke is notified of a successful transaction its team will begin processing the order.
Selection Process

The list of genres provided will be the only factor in determining the content of an individual's order. Your privacy will remain. Choke will pick from within the specified list and compile a collection ready for shipping.

Shipping

Choke - Packaging and Processing: 2-5 days
USPS - Media Mail: 2-8 days

Estimated Delivery Time: 12 days.

Note: Choke will delivery directly should the delivery address be within a 15 mile radius of its office location in Mercedes, Tx. In which case delivery times will vary between 1-5 days.

New or Used?

Completely assembled through second-hand sources. Used discs, used record, used tape. Everything that leaves the facility is guaranteed to meet this standard of quality.

Not profit-seeking but charge $20?
Choke excersises a "profit-second" model of transaction. Little emphasis is placed on revenue. $20 covers expenses with enough left over to reinvest into itself. A detailed breakdown of its resource allocation is made available for anyone to see.
